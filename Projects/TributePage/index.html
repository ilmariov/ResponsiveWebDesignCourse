Sir George Harrison

The man who have touched billions of hearts through his music

Here is a timeline of George's life:

  • 1943 - Born in Liverpool England.

  • 1958 - Paul Mccartney and John Lennon introduced george to the band "The Quarrymen"

  • 1960 - Harrison's music career was in full swing. Lennon had renamed the band the Beatles.Before the end of 1962, Harrison and the Beatles recorded a top 20 U.K.

  • 1963 - From the start, the Beatles were a Lennon-McCartney driven band and brand. But while the two took up much of the group's songwriting responsibilities, Harrison had shown an early interest in contributing his own work. In the summer of 1963 he spearheaded his first song, "Don't Bother Me,"

  • 1968 - George's interest in Indian music leads to the Beatles' famous entanglement with the Maharishi Mahesh Yogi. By 1968, the Beatles were drifting apart. Both Lennon and McCartney antagonise Harrison who walks off the set of the film Let it Be after an argument

  • 1970 - Harrison is the first Beatle to start a successful solo career, selling 3m copies worldwide with his first album, All Things Must Pass

  • 1973 - Releases the hit solo album Give Me Love (Give Me Peace on Earth)

  • 1979 - George focuses on a new career as a film producer, and forms the company Handmade Films with Dennis O'Brien

  • 1980 - Lennon's death inspires Harrison to write the tribute song All Those Years Ago

  • 1987 - Harrison also put together a new rock group, the Travelling Wilburys, with Bob Dylan, Jeff Lyne, Roy Orbison, and Tom Petty

  • 2001 - Aged 58, he loses his fight against cancer, and the tributes pour in from all over the world
